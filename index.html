Going forward

What does the future hold?

Sep. 06, 2019

I have always taken some sort of pride in what I do in life. The process of creation, and putting something out into the world has always been something that has given me a tremendous sense of achievement. You can see this in things I have done in the past, such as creating my own .webs.com website, or trying to learn C++ with my highschool frend so that I may create my own video games. This passion still runs deep in my soul. I still want to create something, be it a podcast, another website, a portfolio, or whatever else I can think of. I want to turn these things into a career that I can actually enjoy. I think that what I really need to do is to wait and learn different things and try as many things as I can, so that I can decide what I want to do in my own time, and enjoy the process along then way, living free of anxiety and stress, and my own compulsive nature of wanting to do everything all at once.
